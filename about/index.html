I am Vincent, and I am finishing a PhD at the Physico-Chimie Curie lab on causal graph reconstruction from observational data of biological or clinical nature under the supervision of Hervé Isambert.

During my thesis, I worked on an information-theoretic constraint-based approach to infer causality from mixed data of any type, to be able to deal with heterogeneous real-life datasets like eletronic medical records (1).

I also contributed with my colleagues to make this class of algorithms more interpretable and more reliable (2, 3).

My research interests are Causal Graphs and Causal Inference, Information Theory, and mostly everything that uses conditional independence testing.
